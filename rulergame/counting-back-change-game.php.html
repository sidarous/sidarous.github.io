The Counting Back Change Game - Learn to Give Change

Instructions:

  • Use this game to learn how to give the right amount of change to a customer.
  • This game can be played using a mouse or a touchscreen.
  • Press the Play Game button to begin playing the game.
  • At the top, the game tells you how much the purchase costs. Below, it shows what kind of bills and coins the buyer gives you. You'll have to count how much money the buyer gives you and then calculate how much money you will have to give them back.
  • With your mouse or finger, click/tap and hold the bills and coins below the white play area and drag them one at a time to the white play area.
  • When you have moved the correct amount of money to the play area, click/tap the "Submit Answer" button below.
  • For each correct answer, you will be awarded points. Your score is displayed on the left side of the Results Bar.
  • Points are awarded based on the following table.
    LevelTime LimitPoints
    130 seconds10
    226 seconds20
    322 seconds30
    418 seconds40
    514 seconds50
    610 seconds60
    76 seconds70
    82 seconds80
  • Each time you correctly answer 5 questions, you will advance to the next level. Your current level is shown in the Results Bar.
  • The timer is represented to the right of the score in the Results Bar. As time passes, the bar will get shorter.
  • If you have not submitted your answer when the time runs out, you will receive a strike.
  • Also, for each incorrect answer, you will receive a strike. After three strikes, the game is over. The number of strikes you have received is indicated on the right side of the Results Bar.
  • Click the Settings button to adjust your preferences for the game.
    • Timer: Turn Off if you want to have unlimited time to submit your answer.
    • Sounds: Turn Off to disable all game sounds.
    • Change From: By default, "Both" is selected, which means the game includes bills and coins. If you select "Small Money," the game will include only coins and one-dollar bills. If you select "Big Money," the game will include only bills.
    • All the settings will revert to default, and your score will reset if you refresh your browser.
